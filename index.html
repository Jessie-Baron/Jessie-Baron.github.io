About

My story growing up is one of hard work and persistence in the face of uncertainty and strife. I am the son of a working class single-father and growing up, I understood that my success, and my survival, were mostly on my shoulders.

In 2018, I became the first member of my family to graduate from a 4-year University. I did this while balancing a full-time job taking most of my classes virtually, spending my days behind a bar and my nights studying for exams.

I got my start as a developer in 2021 as an Email Developer for a top ranked fundraising firm in Washington DC. After 6 months of building out highly responsive and interactive fundraising emails in HTML and CSS, I enrolled in App Academy, an intensive, 1,000 hour coding bootcamp with just a 3% acceptance rate. There I learned new technologies like JavaScript, React, Express.js and more! I used those skills to build some of the most sophisticated applications ever built by a single developer.
